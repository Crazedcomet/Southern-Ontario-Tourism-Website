National Gallery of Canada

The National Gallery is home to thousands of peices of art from around Canada. It also includes special collections of indigenous artworks. It is one of the most famous art museums in the country!

Canada Aviation and Space Museum

Visit the official museum for all things air and space! The large complex features many fighter jets, rescue aircraft, rocketry, satellites and more. The museum is located just outside Ottawa and is a short car/bus ride from downtown!
