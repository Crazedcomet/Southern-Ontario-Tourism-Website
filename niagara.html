Awesome Attractions

Niagara Falls

The Niagara Falls include a wide area of 3 different falls on the Canadian and American sides. Some activities include boat tours, ziplining and hiking. Visit the official Niagara Parks website to buy official tickets!

Learn More

Clifton Hill

Clifton Hill is the perfect entertainement and restaurant spot for famillies and adults alike! Enjoy activities sucha as mini golf, ferris wheel rides, maze rooms and more! Enjoy casual and fine dining on the hill as well!

Learn More

Niagara-On-The-Lake

This little town situated at the edge of Lake Ontario provides some great shopping and dinning experience. With over two centuries of history, be sure to check out the famous buildings like the Princess Wales Hotel!

Transportation

WEGO Bus

The WEGO Bus system is run by Niagara Parks and is a great way to geat around the Niagara Falls area. It can take you to major attractions like The Falls, Clifton Hill, Laura Secord Homestead, The Flower Clock and more. They opperate many lines at different times of the year Check out their website for pricing and route information!
